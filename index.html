Akan name generator!

This web application generators your Akan name based on the birthday details you provide.Akan names were used by the Akan people of Ghana to name their children. The names are also days of the week but are different in both males and females.

The Akan people of Ghana.

If you would like to know your Akan name , follow the steps below :

  1. Go the form.
  2. Fill in your the day,month and year you were born.
  3. Select your gender.
  4. Click the submit button.
  5. Refresh the page to try another date!!
